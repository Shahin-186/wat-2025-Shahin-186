Planning & Zoning

Information about planning applications, zoning rules and guidance for developers and residents.

Common fees & charges

  • Household planning application fee: £173
  • Full planning application (major developments): from £462
  • Pre-application advice (per meeting): £120–£450 depending on scale

How to apply

  1. Prepare your drawings and documentation.
  2. Use the online form or contact the planning office.
  3. Pay the application fee online or at the council offices.

Example: small extension

Typical timescale: 8–12 weeks. Typical fee: £173. You may also need building regulation approval.
