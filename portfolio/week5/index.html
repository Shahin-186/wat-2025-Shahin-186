lemon custard tarts

Ingredients

For the pastry:
  • 90g butter
  • 45g caster sugar
  • grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 120g plain flour
For the filling:
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 egg yolks
  • juice of 4 or 5 lemons (about 150ml)
  • 190g caster sugar
  • 60g unsalted butter
  • optional gooseberry jam to glaze

Other method

Make the pastry:
  1. Mix butter, sugar and lemon zest together
  2. Add egg yolk and mix well
  3. Sift in flour and mix to a dough
  4. Wrap in cling film and chill for 45 minutes
  5. Roll out dough on floured surface and line tart tins
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C and blind bake for 15 minutes
Make the filling:
  1. Whisk eggs, egg yolks, lemon juice and sugar together in a bowl until well combined
  2. Pour mixture into a saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened (do not boil) - about 15 minutes
  3. Remove from heat and stir in butter until melted and smooth
  4. Pour filling into the baked tart shells
  5. Once cooled, chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours
  6. Optional: brush tops with warmed gooseberry jam for a glossy finish
  7. For a caramelized top, sprinkle with sugar and use a kitchen blowtorch to melt and brown the sugar
