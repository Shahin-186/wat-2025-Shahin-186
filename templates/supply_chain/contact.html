Contact us

Find a contact number or online form on the relevant web page.

Report abuse or neglect

  • Report a child protection concern
    If a child is at immediate risk, call 999. For non‑urgent concerns use the online safeguarding form or phone the safeguarding team.
  • Help for adults being abused or neglected
    If an adult is at immediate risk, call 999. For non‑urgent adult safeguarding contact the adult social care team.

Send us a message

You can send us a message on Facebook or Twitter about most enquiries between 9am and 5pm, Monday to Friday. It's usually quicker than phoning.

  • Facebook: /TheDigitalCouncil
  • Twitter: @TheDigitalCouncil_Help

Phone us

  • Before you call: check whether you can complete your task online to save time.
  • Apply, report or pay online: use the website to apply, report or pay for services.
  • General number: 0111 444 5555
  • Lines are open Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm (Wednesdays from 10am).

If you are deaf, have hearing loss or find speaking difficult

  • We offer British Sign Language (BSL) video calls.
  • Text relay calling is available — request this service when you contact us.

If you do not speak English

  • Ask for an interpreter when you call and we will arrange translation or interpreting support.

Emergencies and out of hours

  • If you are in danger call 999.
  • For urgent matters outside office hours phone one of our emergency numbers.

Give us feedback

  • You can make a complaint or tell us how our services are doing — Give us your feedback.
